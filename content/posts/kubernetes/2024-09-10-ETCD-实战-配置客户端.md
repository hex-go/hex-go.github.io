## 环境

测试环境为rke2搭建的K8S集群，etcd集群为staticPod的形式运行在Master{1-3}节点

1. 通过cdebug获取etcd容器中的etcdctl；

2. 获取etcd配置`/var/lib/rancher/rke2/server/db/etcd/config`

3. 从配置文件可知CA\KEY\CERT文件路径，以及entrypoint

```bash
alias etcdctl='etcdctl --endpoints=https://MasterHostIP-1:2379,https://MasterHostIP-2:2379,https://MasterHostIP-3:2379 --cert=/var/lib/rancher/rke2/server/tls/etcd/server-client.crt --key=/var/lib/rancher/rke2/server/tls/etcd/server-client.key --cacert=/var/lib/rancher/rke2/server/tls/etcd/server-ca.crt'
```
